This app relies
on donations

We are scientists. We built this app out of our love of science. We believe there are a lot of people out there who love science just like us!

Our goals are, to:

- make it easy for anyone to contribute to science.
- speed up scientific discovery by making it easy for the scientist to recruit participants
- sponsor new science
- do so in a just and fair way.

We fund this app via donation and by charging a fee for business related research. For example, you can choose to donate to us some of the money scientists reimburse you for doing their research.
